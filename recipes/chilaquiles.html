Chilaquiles Rojos

Description

This is an authentic Mexican dish first made by the Aztecs. The name chilaquiles comes from the Aztec language Nahuatl and translates to chilis and greens.

There are different types of chilaquiles. Some are made with red sauce and some with green sauce. The recipe below is for red sauce chilaquiles (non-spicy but can be).

Ingredients

  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 1/2 White onion
  • 2 Chile guajillo
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano (dried or fresh)
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of consome
  • 10 corn tortillas
  • 1/2 a cup cooking oil
  • Queso fresco

Steps

  1. Cut tortillas into small triangles
  2. Fry the tortillas until golden brown and remove them and place them on a paper towel to cool
  3. Boil the tomatoes, onions, chile guajillo and oregano together until soft
  4. remove the boiled ingredients and place them on the blender
  5. Add a tablespoon of salt and a teaspoon of Consume and blend all boiled ingredients together for one minute
  6. Pour blended sauce into a frying pan with a tablespoon of cooking oil and let it simmer for a minute
  7. Add the fried tortillas into the pan with the sauce and stir them until they are fully covered
  8. Serve and add the queso fresco to your liking, enjoy!
